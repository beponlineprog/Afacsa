About Us

Who We Are

Apostolic Faith Assembly of Africa is a Bible-believing church committed to preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ, making disciples, building families, and transforming communities through the power of the Holy Spirit.


Vision

To build a Christ-centred church that transforms lives and prepares believers for God's Kingdom.


Mission

  • Preach the Gospel
  • Make Disciples
  • Develop Leaders
  • Build Strong Families
  • Serve Our Communities
